How to Verify Email

Follow these easy steps to find your verification link:
(Nang·ni verification link-ko am·na ia step-rangko ja·rikbo:)

1
Open your Email App (like Gmail, Yahoo, etc.)
Nang·ni Email App-ko on ka·bo (Gmail, Yahoo, gipinrang)
2
Look for an email from Garo AI or noreply.
Garo AI ba noreply-oni email-ko am·bo.
3
Can't find it? Check your Spam or Junk folder!
Man·jaengama? Nang·ni Spam ba Junk folder-ko ninaibo!
4
Open the email and click the verification link inside.
Email-ko on ka·bo aro ning·o donggipa verification link-ko click ka·bo.
Note: We use a secure magic link instead of a typed code. Just click the link to verify!
(Chinga code-ni pal magic link-ko jakkala. Verify ka·na link-ko click ka·aibo!)
